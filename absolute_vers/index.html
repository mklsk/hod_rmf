Ruzhanskaya

Furniture  factory

Ruzhanskaya furniture factory was founded in September 1944.

Currently it equipped with the modern technological equipment of the leading Italian brands Comec, Biesse, Selko, Artech, Superfici, Azzura, Italpress with the network of special stores across the country.
